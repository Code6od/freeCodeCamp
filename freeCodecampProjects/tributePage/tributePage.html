The following is Bill's life and his accomplishments

  • 28 October 1955- He was born in Seattle, Washington to Henry Gates II and Mary Gates
  • 1967- Goes to Lakeside School
  • 1973- Enrolls at Havard University
  • 1975- Dropsout of school. Starts Microsoft with Paul
  • 1980- Microsoft signs an important deal with IBM
  • 1987- Becomes youngest-ever billionaire
  • 1994- Marries a Microsoft employee Melinda French
  • 2000- Starts Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation
  • 2007- Gets an honorary degree from Havard
